This visualisation contains two charts from the domain of Oil and Energy Consumption. The two visual idioms used here are, Choropleth Map & Line Chart.

Oil Consumption Around the globe (TWh)

This visualisation shows us the oil consumption of every country in the world with diverging set of colours and with varying luminance. As it can be seen, there are only a handful of countries with an oil consumption below 100. Majority of the countries have an oil consumption close to 1000 TWh.

What is the oil consumption of your country ?

Co2 Emissions Around The Globe from 1970 to 2018

From the shown line chart, it is quite clear that the Co2 emissions have been on a steady rise. The world has seen a very short reduction is Co2 emissions few times since 1970, but there has not been a single reduction in nearly 12 years!! The reason of this dramatic increase is simply because of the fossil fuels that are being burnt around the world for energy.
